ABOUT KEMIRA INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD.

Kemira International (Pvt) Ltd is the pioneer in Innovative Agri-input sector in Sri Lanka. Our company was founded by Mrs. Kumari Yatawara, who continues to spearhead the company’s activities as its Chairman. Our Board constitutes of professionally qualified individuals coupled with a wealth of international experience in Agri-input trade. A highly dynamic, motivated and goal-oriented team of staff members supports our Board. As one of Sri Lanka’s leading companies, Kemira has the ability to adapt with the customers and the changing operating environment. In this context, Kemira leverages its significant prowess in research to successfully anticipate customers’ needs and wants which are constantly evolving, and to offer products and services, exceeding the expectations of the customers.  Company has made a significant impact in the local agriculture landscape with the extensive research carried out with the farming community. We have capitalized the opportunities presented as a result of research and development and productivity improvement in agriculture. We strive to attain success in ways that reflect the integrity of our organization to the outside world and people who have business dealings with us. We maintain high standards of professionalism in our dealings and accountability in all of our business transactions.
