About Kemira





Kemira International (Pvt) Ltd is the pioneer in Innovative Agri-input sector in Sri Lanka. Our company was founded by Mrs. Kumari Yatawara, who continues to spearhead the company’s activities as its Chairman. Our Board constitutes of professionally qualified individuals coupled with a wealth of international experience in Agri-input trade. A highly dynamic, motivated and goal-oriented team of staff members supports our Board.
